## 3) First weekly post — May 1–3, 2026

**This weekend near Naperville: May 1–3**

A few easy picks for the first weekend of May:

**Best date-night energy:**
First Fridays Aurora — art, music, galleries, pop-ups, and downtown wandering. Fri evening.
https://www.auroradowntown.org/first-fridays

**Best market stroll:**
Wheaton French Market — flowers, food, gifts, and low-commitment Saturday morning browsing. Sat, typically 8–2.
https://www.wheaton.il.us/1004/French-Market

**Best Sunday reset:**
Geneva French Market — good excuse for coffee/brunch + a little downtown Geneva walk. Sun, typically 9–2.
https://www.genevachamber.com/geneva-french-market

**Best easy local classic:**
Naperville Riverwalk — free, pretty, and close to food before/after.
https://www.napervilleparks.org/riverwalk

**Best kid backup:**
DuPage Children’s Museum — indoor, hands-on, low weather risk.
https://dupagechildrens.org/visit/

**Worth checking before you go:** hours/weather can shift, especially for outdoor/seasonal spots.

Forward this to the person who says they want plans but never picks anything.

## 4) Starter post — rainy-day saves

**Rainy-day saves near Naperville**

For the weekends when Illinois weather ruins the cute outdoor plan:

- **DuPage Children’s Museum** — best for younger kids who need to burn energy indoors.
  https://dupagechildrens.org

- **Hollywood Palms** — dine-in movie night with less planning required.
  https://www.hollywoodpalmscinema.com

- **WhirlyBall Naperville** — group-friendly chaos: WhirlyBall, bowling, games, food.
  https://www.whirlyball.com/naperville/

- **Topgolf Naperville** — covered bays, snacks, and a safe “we did something” plan.
  https://topgolf.com/us/naperville/

- **Round1 at Fox Valley Mall** — arcade/bowling/claw-machine rabbit hole.
  https://www.round1usa.com

Save this for the next gray Saturday.

## 5) Starter post — worth the drive

**Worth-the-drive ideas**

When you want to leave the house but not commit to Chicago traffic:

- **Morton Arboretum, Lisle** — trails, spring color, Children’s Garden, date walk potential.
  https://mortonarb.org/visit-the-arboretum/

- **Cantigny Park, Wheaton** — gardens, walking, museums, easy family afternoon.
  https://cantigny.org/visit/

- **Paramount Theatre, Aurora** — big-theater night out without going downtown Chicago.
  https://paramountaurora.com

- **Wonderverse, Oak Brook** — indoor games/immersive attractions + food/drinks.
  https://www.wonderversechicago.com

- **Mitsuwa Marketplace, Arlington Heights** — food court + Japanese market mini-trip.
  https://mitsuwa.com/ar/

This channel is for plans that feel easier than endless scrolling.
