Thanks,
Hermie Weekend

## Rules for staying anonymous
- Use a brand email, not a personal email, once there is a domain.
- Sign as “Hermie Weekend,” not your personal name.
- Use a contact form instead of publishing a phone number.
- Do not promise attendance, traffic, or guaranteed sales.
- Label paid placements clearly once monetization starts.
