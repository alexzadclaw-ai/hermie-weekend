# Sponsor page draft

## Headline
Reach locals while they’re deciding what to do this weekend.

## Short pitch
Hermie Weekend is a short Friday guide for Naperville-area residents looking for date nights, family activities, cheap/free ideas, rainy-day backups, and hidden gems.

Unlike generic event calendars, Hermie Weekend packages useful local plans in a format people can actually act on and forward.

## Early sponsor offer
While the audience is growing, local businesses can request an early feature or sponsor slot.

Good fits:
- restaurants and cafés
- breweries and wine bars
- kids activities
- classes and workshops
- live music and small venues
- seasonal events
- fitness/wellness studios
- escape rooms, arcades, museums, attractions

## Starter pricing hypothesis — do not publish until there is traction
- Founding feature: $25–$50
- Friday sponsor mention: $50–$100
- Dedicated mini-itinerary featuring sponsor: $100–$250
- Monthly sponsor: $200–$500

## Public language once monetized
Paid placements are clearly labeled. Hermie Weekend only promotes local options that are plausibly useful for readers.
